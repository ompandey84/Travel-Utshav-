About Travel Utsav

Your premier adventure travel partner based in Indore, Madhya Pradesh

Our Story

Travel Utsav was founded in 2020 by a group of passionate travelers from Indore who wanted to share the beauty of Madhya Pradesh and beyond with the world. What started as a small local tour operation has quickly grown into a comprehensive adventure travel company offering unique experiences across India.

Based in the heart of India - Indore, Madhya Pradesh - we take pride in showcasing both the popular destinations and hidden gems of our country. Our founders' deep knowledge of local culture, geography, and history allows us to create authentic travel experiences that connect travelers with the true essence of each destination.

Over the years, we've expanded our offerings to include a wide range of adventure activities, cultural tours, spiritual journeys, and custom travel experiences for individuals, families, and groups. Our team of experienced guides, travel planners, and adventure specialists work tirelessly to ensure that every journey with Travel Utsav becomes a cherished memory.

Our Mission & Values

At Travel Utsav, our mission is to provide unforgettable travel experiences that inspire, educate, and transform. We believe that travel has the power to change lives, broaden perspectives, and create meaningful connections between people and places.

Sustainability

We are committed to responsible tourism practices that protect the environment, support local communities, and preserve cultural heritage for future generations.

Authenticity

We create genuine experiences that reflect the true character of each destination, connecting travelers with local cultures, traditions, and people.

Safety

The safety and wellbeing of our customers is our top priority. We maintain rigorous safety standards and provide comprehensive training for all our guides and staff.

Passion

Our team's love for travel and adventure shines through in everything we do. We're driven by a desire to share our passion and create extraordinary experiences for our customers.
